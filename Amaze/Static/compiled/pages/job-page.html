Creative Director

Manchester / Permanant

Posted on 16 February 2015

We are seeking a Creative Director who is capable of managing a multi-million pound portfolio and working at the highest level across numerous innovative clients.

Working in partnership with the Senior Creative Director, you will share responsibility for your own P&L, production team and Accounts. Working across a number of established accounts and new business opportunities, your role will be hugely influential in defining and driving the success of the agency.

About you

A respected leader, coach and consultant - you are either already, or feel that you are now ready, to lead and manage a multi-million P&L / client portfolio. You have a passion for digital and are seen by your colleagues as a digital native. You have experience across new business, estimating and complex deliveries. You will be from an agency or SI background and have spent many years building a reputation as a delivery expert. You are a skilled client and people manager who thrives under the demands of a large team, delivering across multiple time zones and clients. You are looking for an agency where you will have the autonomy to define your own success, and are seeking a role which allows you to influence the future direction of your team was well as the wider agency.
