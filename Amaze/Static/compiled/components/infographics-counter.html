Counter Three

How to Apply?

The Amaze Graduate Programme launched in August 2013 and is proving a great success.

It’s been set up with the aim of developing an innovative, employer led scheme to attract and retain graduate talent within Amaze - a leading full service digital agency with global reach.

The programme is designed to support work based learning, undertaken through placements within 5 separate areas of our business for a 12 month period. If successful, there will also be the opportunity for you to move into a permanent role once the 12 months is completed.

  • Applicants must have finished their degree
  • Applicants will be working in our Manchester office
  • Available to start on 1st September 2015
  • You must be eligible to work in the UK
